UnseenCode: Invisible On-screen Barcode with Image-based Extraction

Group Menbers:罗嘉鸣、陈炳昊、刘龙祥、张米志鹏

Introduction

The background of our project is as follows

We hope to design a new communication method. For this new communication method, we have the following requirements, which are not visible to the human eye, can carry a sufficient amount of information, and have low hardware requirements

If we can successfully design such a product, its practical significance is also very obvious, especially for applications such as electronic advertising, it will be free to design patterns and then embed data. So this work is also more important.

    • Principle

    Illustration of UnseenCode system design

    The encoding end first converts the picture into the CIE color space, divides the picture, and then embeds 0 or 1 for the X component of each block, that is, generates a corresponding frame pair, and finally converts back to the RGB space for alternate display

    The decoder also first divides the frame, converts it into CIE space, then calculates △ X, and finally compares △ X with the templates of 0 and 1 to determine the bits contained in the current block.

    Barcode templates to represent one bit

    We use two barcodes to represent 0 and 1 respectively
